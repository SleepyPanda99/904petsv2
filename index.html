About Us

Joshua Predieri established 904 Pets LLC in 2012.

Josh is a Navy Veteran, UNF Graduate, a Jaguars fan and of course an animal lover! He has gained many clients and friends across the 904 area; pets and people alike. Every animal he meets immediately falls in love because he treats them like the Kings and Queens they are!

Liscensed, bonded and insured - you know your pets are in the right hands when they are with Josh. Services include overnight pet sitting, dog walking and daily pet check-ins.
